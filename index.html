Greater Manchester Students' Assembly

WHAT?

The Greater Manchester Students' Assembly (GMSA) is a student led body collectively representing over 100,000 students.

WHO

Membership is made up of the five students' unions within Greater Manchester.

WHAT DO WE DO

The GMSA works on and represents its membership on issues that affect students daily.

GET INVOLVED

If you are a student in Manchester and would like to get involved, please speak to your students' union executive
