Greater Manchester Students' Assembly

  • WHAT?

    The Greater Manchester Students' Assembly (GMSA) is a student led body collectively representing over 100,000 students in Greater Manchester (GM).

  • WHO ARE WE

    Membership is made up of the five students' unions within Greater Manchester.

  • WHAT DO WE DO

    The GMSA works on and represents its membership on issues that affect students daily.
